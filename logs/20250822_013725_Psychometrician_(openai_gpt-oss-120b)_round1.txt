- **Psychological Manipulation** captures covert, cognitive‑distorting tactics (gaslighting, devaluation, self‑harm threats) that are conceptually different from the more overt emotional or physical abuse items.

These seven latent dimensions provide a parsimonious, non‑overlapping representation of the underlying constructs reflected in the item pool.
