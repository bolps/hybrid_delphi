### **1. Coercive Control and Isolation**
*Label:* **Systematic Domination Through Surveillance, Possession, and Social Restriction**

- Mi chiede di non vedere i miei amici/amiche

**Rationale:**

The dimension includes digital surveillance (“troppi messaggi”) and jealousy-based demands, all serving to isolate and subordinate. It aligns with Stark’s (2007) concept of “coercive control” as a pattern of domination, not isolated incidents.
