**Latent dimension 2 – Emotional / Verbal Abuse (devaluation, criticism, blame, gaslighting, humiliation)**  
*Rationale:* These items involve attacks on the partner’s self‑esteem, frequent criticism, blaming, and tactics that distort reality (gaslighting). They are the classic verbal‑emotional abuse markers that undermine the victim’s sense of worth and safety.
