*   Mi dice che non valgo niente e che resterei sola se non stessi con lui perché nessuno mi vorrebbe

The items describe verbal abuse, gaslighting, emotional volatility, excessive demands for loyalty, and the instillation of fear or self-doubt. The core theme is the psychological degradation and control exerted by one person over another, often through non-physical means.
